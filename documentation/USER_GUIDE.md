# User Guide

## Welcome, Agent.

**MATAJALAN_OS** is a community-driven surveillance network. Your contributions help make the roads safer.

## Getting Started

### 1. Registration
Create an account to begin your service.
- **Tier 2 (Basic)** access is granted immediately.
- You can browse the vehicle database and submit basic reports.

### 2. Identity Verification (KYC)
To upgrade to **Tier 1** clearance:
1. Go to **KYC Verification** in the dashboard.
2. Select your document type (Passport, ID Card, Driving License).
3. Upload a clear scan or photo (JPG, PNG, PDF).
4. Wait for Admin approval (usually 24-48 hours).

**Benefits of Tier 1:**
- Higher Trust Score for your reports.
- Access to detailed vehicle history.
- "Verified" badge on your profile.

## Core Features

### Surveillance Grid (Home)
- **Search**: Enter a license plate (e.g., `B 1234 XYZ`) to find a target.
- **High Threats**: View vehicles with a low safety score (< 40/100).
- **Live Feed**: Real-time ticker of recent reports.

### Submitting a Report
1. Search for the vehicle or click "Submit Report" on a vehicle's profile.
2. **Rate**: 1 Star (Critical Threat) to 5 Stars (Safe).
3. **Comment**: Describe the incident (e.g., "Ran a red light at intersection X").
4. **Tags**: Add keywords like `speeding`, `aggressive`, `illegal_parking`.

### Dashboard
Your personal command center.
- **Status**: Check your clearance level.
- **History**: View your submitted reports.
- **Settings**: Update your profile and password.

## Security Protocols
- **Do not share your credentials.**
- **Report responsibly.** False reporting may lead to account termination.
